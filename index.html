Our Story

Taste the Tradition of Hyderabad

Located in the heart of Secunderabad, Tandoor Restaurant brings you the authentic flavors of Indian cuisine. Whether you are craving the rich spices of a Hyderabadi Biryani or a comforting cup of chai, we have something for everyone.

We pride ourselves on providing high-quality, delicious Veg and Non-Veg dishes at affordable prices. From quick lunches for office goers to relaxed family dinners, our warm ambiance and dedicated service ensure a memorable dining experience.
